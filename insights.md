\# 📊 Olist E-commerce Analysis — Key Insights



This project analyzes customer behavior, sales trends, delivery performance, and payment patterns using SQL.



\## 📈 Monthly Revenue Trend



Monthly revenue shows noticeable fluctuations with an overall gradual upward trend. This suggests the presence of seasonal demand patterns, where certain months experience higher sales peaks. The consistent growth over time indicates improving business performance and increasing customer engagement.



\## 🚚 Delivery Performance



A portion of orders were delivered later than the estimated delivery date, indicating inefficiencies in the logistics process. The average delay suggests that while most deliveries are timely, there are consistent delays affecting customer experience. This highlights an opportunity for improving supply chain and delivery operations.



\## 💳 Payment Behavior



Credit card is the most used payment method, contributing the highest share of revenue, indicating strong customer preference. This indicates a strong customer preference for card-based transactions. Other payment methods contribute comparatively less, suggesting opportunities to improve adoption through incentives or better integration. Understanding this distribution helps the business optimize payment strategies and enhance user experience.



\## 📦 Product \& Sales Insights



Certain product categories generated significantly higher revenue and order volume compared to others. Sales contribution was uneven across categories, indicating that a small group of products drives a major portion of overall business revenue. This helps identify high-performing product segments and supports better inventory and marketing decisions.



\## 🌍 Regional Sales Analysis



Revenue contribution varied across different states and cities, showing that customer demand is geographically concentrated in specific regions. Some locations consistently generated higher sales and order activity, helping identify strong markets for business expansion and targeted promotions.



\## 🧠 Customer RFM Segmentation



RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis was performed to segment customers based on purchasing behavior. Customers with recent purchases, higher order frequency, and greater spending were identified as high-value customers. This segmentation helps businesses improve retention strategies, customer targeting, and personalized marketing campaigns.
